Brand Design

Eftee Organics - Crafting a Timeless Identity for Sustainable Lagomorph Farming


1. Mission: Ethical, Modern, Community-Focused

Eftee Organics is a Zimbabwe-based commercial rabbitry operating within an outgrower framework. The brand needed a visual identity that could speak to two distinct worlds: the local family-run kitchen and the high-standard procurement offices of national meat retailers.


CHALLENGE:

Most agricultural branding in the region relies on literal, busy illustrations of animals. Eftee Organics required a "Full-Stack" approach to its identity—something that felt as natural on a farm gate as it did as a digital app icon for management software.


2. Concept: The Sprout & The Silhouette

The Eftee Organics icon was born from a desire for a symbolic mark rather than a literal drawing. The resulting "dual-concept" icon serves as the heart of the brand.


Anatomy:

A thick circular ring representing the "closed-loop" nature of organic farming.


Dual Meaning:

Emerging from the center are two leaf-like extensions. At first glance, they are the alert ears of a rabbit; at second glance, they are the first two leaves of a sprouted seed.


Symbiosis:

This symbolizes the relationship between the rabbit and the earth—where the farm's "waste" (organic fertilizer) fuels the "growth" of the crop.


3. Visual Language: Stability & Nature

COLOUR PALETTE

  • Deep Bluish-Green: Represents health, organic vitality, and a modern twist on traditional agricultural greens.

  • Dark Grey & Pure White: Used for the typography and backgrounds to provide high-contrast, professional clarity, and a sense of "cleanliness" essential for food production.

TYPOGRAPHY

The choice of a Medium-Bold Serif font provides a bridge between the traditional reliability of farming and the sophisticated precision of a tech-forward business. The serifs add a "timeless" quality, ensuring the brand remains respectable for decades.


4. Versatility: From Soil to Screen

A key requirement was that the logo must function across multiple platforms. Whether it is a small product sticker or a digital interface, the thick lines and simple geometry ensure legibility.


5. The Impact: The Trust Deficit Solved

The final identity for Eftee Organics moves away from the "backyard" aesthetic and toward an "industrial-organic" standard. When a retailer sees this mark on a product or a website, the message is clear:

'This production is thorough, professional, and built for the future of the new economy.'
